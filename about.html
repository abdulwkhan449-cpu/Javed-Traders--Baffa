Our Story

FROM A SMALL SHOP TO BAFFA'S TRUSTED GROCERY DESTINATION

Javed Traders was founded in 1985 by Muhammad Javed with a simple vision: to provide the people of Baffa and the wider Pakhal region with the highest quality household and grocery items at the most affordable prices.

What started as a small corner shop at Main Chowk has grown into a premier retail destination, thanks to decades of unwavering trust, integrity, and a deep understanding of the community's needs. From fresh kiryana staples to premium cleaning essentials, every product on our shelves is chosen with care.

Today, the legacy continues under the leadership of Usman Javed, who has modernized the business with a digital showroom while preserving the traditional values that made Javed Traders a household name.
